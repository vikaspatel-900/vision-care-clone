  • Meet Our Speicalist

    The philosophy of Vision Care and Research Centre Eye Hospital is to provide honest, genuine, and the latest treatment to all patients at an affordable cost. The hospital’s vision is to become a leading centre of excellence in eye care services by delivering high-quality, ethical, and patient-centric care.

    Dr. Gajendra Chawla

    EYE RETINA – LASER SPECIALIST

    Dr. Gajendra Chawla

    DIRECTOR & EYE RETINA – LASER SPECIALIST, CHAIRPERSON VISION CARE AND RESEARCH CENTRE

    Dr Gajendra Chawla , director of vision care & research centre Bring 15 bedded exclusive eye hospital, he passed his master degree in ophthalmology in 1991 from Gandhi Medical College Bhopal. He underwent 2 year fellowship training programme in vitreo retinal surgery from world’s premium institute Sankara nethralaya Chennai from 1991 to 1993

    After completion of fellowship, he worked as vitreo retinal consultant at Venu eye institute New Delhi. In 1996 he joined Bhopal eye hospital Bhopal as vitreo retinal consultant. He has key pioneered vitreo retinal surgery & laser in Bhopal. In sept. 2000 Dr Chawla become visiting vitreo retinal consultant to various hospitals at Bhopal including Ajwani eye hospital, sewasadan eye hospital, mata mandir netra sewa Kendra, apart from his own clinic. Finally his dream came true on 16th march 2008 when 10-beded exclusive eye hospital Vision Care & research centre started functioning.

    Dr Chawla is having good academic career also. He is invited regularly as guest faculty in various ophthalmological conferences including Bhopal divisional ophthalmic society, Indore Divisional ophthalmic society, and MP state ophthalmic society, Delhi ophthalmic society and has delivered talk on subjects. His main area of interest is Diabetic eye disease and retinopathy of prematurity. At present he is The Precident of Bhopal divisional ophthalmic society.

    Dr Chawla is keen to extend his professional services to most needy & weaker section of society who either cannot afford or services are not approachable to them. He is founder chairman of NGO Vision Academy – The socio medical society since its inception in 2008. Under leadership of Dr Chawla, vision academy is involved in creating awareness/screening of diabetic patients in Bhopal and surrounding eight districts including Itarsi, Sehore, Vidisha, Raisen, Sagar, Rajgarh etc.
